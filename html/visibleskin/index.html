Visible Skin ran 10 September 2021 - 18 February 2022 and remains available online here


King's College London, Strand Campus


‘Visible Skin’, funded by the Wellcome Trust, is an exhibition curated by Dr Hannah Murphy, Renaissance Skin Co-I. The work of artist Peter Brathwaite in collaboration with King’s Renaissance Skin research project and King’s Culture, is showcased on this online Gallery, along with the original artwork which it was inspired by. Audio clips from Peter and Hannah take you a step deeper as they speak about different aspects of the exhibition. The About page has all the information about this research project, funders and the people involved, and a Bibliography provides sources and suggestions for further reading. The physical exhibition (now finished) was staged in windows across the King's College London Strand Campus, as well as in The Arcade.
